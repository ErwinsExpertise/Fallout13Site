Frequently Asked Questions


Why am I naked?

Make sure you update your loadout for every character BEFORE your first round in the server or upon choosing another faction.


How often are server updates?

We are updating whenever possible and with as much content as we are able to.


How can I donate to the server?

Currently, we do not accept donations at this time, however, advertising and referring the server to your friends is appreciated.


Can I be on the whitelist for Enclave/BoS?

Please apply on #whitelist-application in Discord with the right format to be considered.
